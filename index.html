How to do HP Printer setup
using
Wi-Fi or wired network or USB cable?


Go to 123.hp.com/setup, download the HP printer software, and install it. Choose your connection method (Wi-Fi, wired, or USB), and follow the prompts to set up your printer. The software will guide you through the setup process, ensuring your printer is ready to use.

Steps: HP Printer setup using Wi-Fi or wired network or USB cable

  • Open a web browser and navigate to 123.hp.com/setup.
  • Select your printer model from the list.
  • Download the HP printer software for your device (Windows or Mac).
  • Save the installer to your computer.
  • Choose to connect via Wi-Fi, wired network, or USB cable.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions for your chosen method.
  • Select your Wi-Fi network from the list of available networks.
  • Enter your Wi-Fi network password.
  • For wired, connect the Ethernet cable to your printer and router.
  • For USB, connect the cable to your printer and device.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the setup process.
  • Test your printer to ensure it's working correctly.
  • Print a test page to verify the connection.
  • Ensure the printer is working correctly and connected to your network or device.
